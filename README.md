# Pomit 🍅

Pomit is a modern, progressive Pomodoro timer built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose for Android. It revolutionizes the traditional Pomodoro technique by introducing a **gamified, step-based progression system** that naturally adapts to your growing focus capacity.

## Key Features ✨

- **Progressive Timer:** Instead of fixed 25-minute intervals, Pomit dynamically increases your focus sessions step-by-step, capping at 45 minutes for maximum productivity.
- **Leveling System:** Your current focus duration corresponds to your level (Pelleh). Reach level 45 to earn a Gold Star and unlock infinite peak-focus sessions!
- **Smart Rests:** Rest durations scale with your activity. Don't need a full long rest? Pomit allows you to skip rest sessions after 5 minutes.
- **In-App Updates:** Never miss a new feature. Pomit checks for updates directly from GitHub and installs them seamlessly.
- **Always-On Display:** Prevents your screen from sleeping during active focus sessions.

Built to keep you motivated and productive, Pomit helps you build deep work habits one step at a time! 🚀
